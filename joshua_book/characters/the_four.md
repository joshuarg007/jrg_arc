# The Four

All four begin as Fools—card zero, the step into the void. Their convergence is prophesied and feared. They meet only in twos at first. Forces move to prevent the full gathering.

---

## The Magician

**Arcana**: I - The Magician
*As above, so below. The channel between worlds. Will made manifest.*

### Core Identity
The wolf who was always a wolf, trained to be obedient. Smart kid—spelling bees, poetry contests, knowledge bowl captain. Hands that came from surviving a dangerous brother, not from sports.

He protected her when they were young. Ongoing, across years. Then he was sent away, and the gap opened. Now she's being drained by his brother, who found her because of him.

### The Gift
Changes the actual nature of reality. All four have this ability to some degree—it's the true measure of strength in this world. He's the most developed, the most conscious of it. The Hermetic principle embodied: thought becoming matter, will reshaping what is.

His grandpa's training wasn't just martial arts. It was preparation for this. How to see the cracks. How to survive broken time. How to channel without being consumed.

### The Wound
His brother. The predator he shares blood with. The puppet being driven by something vast, who found the Empress and attached because the Magician's connection to her drew attention.

Everything traces back to him. His presence painted a target. His absence let the feeding begin.

### The Response
Cold necessity. When he discovers what his brother is doing, there's no hesitation. No guilt. Pure action. The wolf knows: some things must be put down. Even if the thing being put down is also a victim.

This isn't cruelty. It's clarity.

### Arc
From obedient wolf to conscious channel. From protector who failed to protector who acts. The lineage died with grandpa—it must be reborn through him. Not inherited. Resurrected.

### Physical/External
- Hispanic
- Had hands before he had training
- Eyes that see more than they should
- Carries himself like someone who learned stillness through violence

### Voice
Direct. Minimal. Says what needs saying. Violence and loyalty in equal measure. Thinks in action, not abstraction.

## The Emperor

**Arcana**: IV - The Emperor
*Structure. Authority. The weight of rule. The father who builds.*

### Core Identity
Built himself from nothing. Hardship-forged. Whatever structure exists in his life, he created it through will and work. Nobody gave him anything.

He carries an unwanted burden. Not chosen. Accepted because someone had to, and he was the one standing there.

### The Gift
Contains a piece of the wrongness itself. His bloodline was bred for this across generations—living prisons, human containment vessels. The wrongness is inside him. Has been since birth.

His gift isn't power in the traditional sense. It's capacity. He can hold what would destroy others. He can carry weight.

### The Wound
The containment is failing. Ticking clock. Slowly, across years that feel like months, he's losing. The wrongness inside is winning. He can feel it spreading, eating at the walls he maintains.

He doesn't know how long he has. Nobody does.

### The Response
Relentless. He didn't choose this, but he doesn't quit. Every day is maintenance. Every hour is holding the line. He builds structure around himself—routines, disciplines, systems—because structure is what keeps the wrongness contained.

He resents the burden. He carries it anyway.

### Arc
From isolated container to connected vessel. The four together might strengthen his containment—or accelerate the failure. The convergence changes everything, for better or worse.

### Physical/External
- Body shows the strain—not sick, but aged beyond years
- Carries himself with control because control is survival
- Eyes that have seen the inside of the wrongness

### Voice
Measured. Deliberate. Doesn't waste words or energy. Everything is conservation. Has dark humor about his situation—gallows jokes about time running out.

## The High Priestess

**Arcana**: II - The High Priestess
*Intuition. Mystery. The keeper of secrets. The veil between worlds.*

### Core Identity
She sees what others miss. Not by choice—the knowledge comes to her. Secrets reveal themselves in her presence. She knows things that could destroy lives, families, institutions.

The burden of knowing is her constant companion.

### The Gift
She perceives the veil's fractures. Where others might sense wrongness, she sees the specific cracks—their shapes, their depths, how much is leaking through. The broken places are her map.

More than perception: she believes she can fix it. That the veil can be healed.

### The Wound
She hopes to repair what chaos has broken. But is this hope or delusion? The story doesn't know yet. She carries the weight of possibly being naive—of dedicating herself to something that might be impossible.

The secrets she carries also isolate her. Knowing too much about everyone makes intimacy difficult.

### The Response
She watches. She waits. She gathers knowledge like others gather weapons. When she acts, it's informed by everything she's seen and understood.

But sometimes knowing isn't enough. Sometimes you have to act without full information. This is where she struggles.

### Arc
From observer to participant. From hoping to testing. The question of whether the veil can be healed will be answered through her—through what she attempts and what results.

### Physical/External
- Stillness that makes people uncomfortable
- Eyes that don't look away when they should
- Often knows what you're going to say before you say it

### Voice
Careful. Precise. Says less than she knows. Questions more than states. When she reveals a secret, it's deliberate—calculated for effect.

## The Empress

**Arcana**: III - The Empress
*Creation. Abundance. Fertility. The mother who nurtures.*

### Core Identity
The girl who needed protection. The Magician guarded her across their childhood—ongoing, multiple ages, a relationship of keeping her safe. When he was sent away, she was exposed.

She gives everything to everyone around her. Nurtures until empty. Caretaker by nature and compulsion.

### The Gift
Creates possibilities—futures that become real. She can manifest what could be into what is. Birth timelines. Will abundance into existence.

At least, she should be able to. The power is her birthright.

### The Wound
She can't manifest. The gift that should be hers doesn't work. She watches others create while she remains blocked, barren of the ability that defines her archetype.

She doesn't know why. She feels the lack—a nameless longing, a sense of something missing—but has no framework for it.

**The truth she'll discover**: The Magician's brother found her. He seduced her—not naturally, but through demonic compulsion. He created false love. And through that connection, he's been feeding on her power. Draining her. Eating the futures she should be birthing.

She loves him. Or believes she does. Can't accept what he is, what he's doing. The denial protects her from horror.

### The Response
When she discovers the truth—that her love is manufactured, that she's being eaten, that the Magician's brother has been parasitizing her potential—something unexpected will happen.

We don't know what yet. Her transformation is a mystery even to us.

### Arc
From drained vessel to... what? The answer emerges through writing. But the arc moves through: discovery of the feeding, breaking the bond, confronting manufactured love, and becoming whatever she was always meant to be.

### Physical/External
- Beauty that draws attention and danger
- Gives off warmth that others lean into
- Shows signs of depletion she can't explain—tired beyond reason, aged in small ways
- Everyone wants something from her; she doesn't know how to stop giving

### Voice
Warm. Present. Attentive to others in a way that can feel like a gift or a trap. Underneath: exhaustion she can't name. Emptiness that shouldn't be there.

## The Four Together

### Connections (to develop)
- **Magician + Empress**: Childhood protector/protected. Separated when he was sent away. Will reunite through the story. He doesn't know what his brother has done to her.
- **Emperor + Priestess**: Unknown yet. Let connections emerge through writing.
- **Cross-pair dynamics**: They meet only in twos before the full convergence. Which pairs meet first will develop organically.

### The Convergence
Their meeting is prophesied across multiple traditions. Different fragments, never the complete picture.

Forces actively prevent it:
- The wrongness itself seems to know
- The hunters serve this prevention without understanding
- The brother's parasitism on the Empress may be strategic—weakening one of the four

What happens when all four converge:
- Unknown
- Possibly healing
- Possibly ending
- What they choose matters more than what they are

### Power Dynamics
All four can change the nature of reality to some degree. The Magician is most developed. Each has additional specific gifts:
- **Magician**: Will-based reality shaping (trained)
- **Emperor**: Containment, carrying what would destroy others
- **Priestess**: Perception of fractures, potential for healing
- **Empress**: Manifestation of possibilities/futures (currently blocked)

Together they might be capable of something none can achieve alone.
